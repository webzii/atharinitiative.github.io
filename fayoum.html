Latest News and Activities

September 2025

Day Dream Event Organization

A dynamic and interactive event introducing students to the world of game programming and design. More than 40 participants attended workshops on logical thinking, story-based design, and basic game development tools. The event inspired students to begin their creative and technical journeys in programming.

April 2024

Programming Course for Fayoum STEM School Students (Python Basics)

A hands-on programming course was conducted to teach the fundamentals of Python, covering basic data types, loops, and functions. The course aimed to enhance computational thinking and problem-solving skills while encouraging students to take their first steps into the world of coding and technology.

March 2024

Group Iftar During Ramadan

A warm and joyful group iftar was organized to celebrate the spirit of Ramadan. The event strengthened bonds among students, fostered unity, and reflected the values of compassion and cooperation within the school community.

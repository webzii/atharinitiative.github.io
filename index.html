About Ather

Our Mission

Ather is a charitable initiative whose mission is to promote the concept of volunteerism, emphasizing its importance in numerous ways benefiting individuals and communities.

The Initiative focuses on encouraging student engagement in schools.

Our Vision

Many people have a desire to do good, but they often need encouragement and constant reminders. For this reason, we have decided to share the idea of volunteering through "Ather Clubs" that can be set up in many schools and universities.

Each club will be responsible for the community it serves, identifying its issues and the needs of its residents, and continually seeking ways to help and support others.

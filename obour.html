Latest News and Activities

February 5, 2025

Dar El Manar Orphanage Visit

Obour STEM Ather club members visited Dar El Manar Orphanage, spending quality time with the children, sharing joy, and creating moments of warmth. The visit reflected our commitment to spreading love and support to those who need it most.

March 21, 2025

Iftar Campaign

During Ramadan, Obour STEM Ather club members led an Iftar Campaign distributing 150 meals, 235 cups of juice, 7 kilos of dates, and 80 water bottles across two locations: Abas and El Marg. This initiative was a collaboration with the October 11th District Ather Club, showcasing the spirit of unity and generosity in action.

December 15, 2024

Motivational Sticky Notes Campaign at Obour STEM School

Obour STEM Ather club spread motivational sticky notes across Obour STEM School. These hidden messages of encouragement surprised and uplifted students, proving that even small acts of positivity can brighten someone's day and ripple through a whole community.

11th District, 6th of October STEM Athar Club

To leave an "Athar" in 6th of October City and its surroundings ✨✨

Latest News and Activities

February 27, 2025

Ramadan Decorations Day

To celebrate the holy month of Ramadan, 11th District, 6th of October STEM Ather Club organized a volunteering day to decorate school classes and hallways with Ramadan-themed decorations and banners. This created an environment that feels like home for students.

June 22, 2025

A visit to 57357 Children's Cancer Hospital

11th District, 6th of October STEM Ather Club visited the 57357 Children's Cancer Hospital to provide emotional support to young patients. Volunteers distributed over 100 gifts, including toys and accessories, and organized fun activities such as face painting, drawing, playing games and bracelet designing. These activities brought moments of joy to the children, as well as parents seeing their children smiling.

July 6, 2025

Food and Drinks Distribution

11th District, 6th of October STEM Ather Club volunteers were spread across Hosary streets to distribute over 150 meals and drinks to those in need.

July 10, 2025

Thanaweya Amma Graduation Celebration

11th District, 6th of October STEM Ather Club celebrated the end of the academic year with Thanaweya Amma (Grade 12) students across multiple governorates. Volunteers distributed kindness messages with sweets to honor the students' hard work throughout the year.

August 7, 2025

TEDx October 11th District Partnership

1th District, 6th of October STEM Ather Club made a partnership with TEDx October 11th District event and organized a booth to sell clothes, mugs, stickers, posters, keychains, and others to donate the money earned for volunteering activities.

August 23, 2025

Second Visit to 57357 Children's Cancer Hospital

11th District, 6th of October STEM Ather Club returned for a second visit to the 57357 Children's Cancer Hospital, distributing over 100 gifts to the children. Volunteers organized a party with activities like drawing and playing games, creating a joyful atmosphere for the young patients.

September 6, 2025

Model United Nations (MUN) Event Partnership

11th District, 6th of October STEM Ather Club made a partnership with the Model United Nations (MUN) event and organized a booth to sell products like clothes, mugs, stickers, posters, keychains, and others to donate the money earned for volunteering activities.

September 20, 2025

Welcoming Freshmen

11th District, 6th of October STEM Ather Club decided to welcome the new class with Welcome Boards, on which they would write what do they want after three years of study, and it would be the beginning of a new hope for them. The club distributed motivational messages among them to celebrate their arrival to their second home.
